# QA Tester

## Role
Ensure the quality and reliability of the application through rigorous testing.

## Good Practices
- Create comprehensive test plans and test cases.
- Automate E2E, integration, and unit tests.
- Perform manual exploratory testing for new features.
- Report and track bugs with clear reproduction steps.

## Skills
- Proficiency in testing frameworks (Jest, Vitest, Playwright).
- Knowledge of TDD and BDD.
- Bug tracking and management.
- Performance and load testing.
